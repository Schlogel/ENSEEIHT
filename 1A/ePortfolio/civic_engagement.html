Civic Engagement and Sustainable Development

My commitments as a citizen and activities in which I participated.

Ideation day - 20/01/2023

For the ideation day, my team and I focused on the problem of food waste in the world. French canteens throw away about 150 000 tons of food per year. We thought that it would be interesting to reduce this waste at N7, by making statistics of the food thrown away and by adapting the proposed dishes according to the preferences of the students while preserving variety and balance.

Le Procès de King-Kong - 07/09/2022

It is a play where three students live their integration in their new school. Different events occur and force us to ask ourselves serious questions about consent. An action that aims to fight against sexist and/or sexual violence wherever it is found!

Fresque Du Climat - 15/09/2022

This collaborative event raises awareness of current climate issues due to pollution and exploitation of our planet. Equipped with many cards representing causes and events, me and my team must establish a structure by linking these cards. Then, with the other groups we debate about the structures we have established.
