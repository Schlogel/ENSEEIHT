Professional Experiences

During my studies I was able to do internships in companies, to discover the professional world and develop new skills.

DiagBot - Entrepreneurship

Initiation day to entrepreneurship : December 9th, 2022

For the entrepreneurship day, my team and I have imagined a platform that aims to accelerate the work of doctors and offer individuals a real-time monitoring of their health by doing a series of simple tests, if they need to consult. This series of tests will be built by an A.I. that collects information on the patient and proposes simple care to do it yourself. A medical file will be established and transmitted to the doctor if the consultation becomes imperative and if the care to be applied becomes complex.

[In'Bô] - Internship

May 2nd - June 10th (2022)

In'Bô is located in the Vosges and is isolated from industrial areas. Its name comes from the Vosges dialect which means "The wood, the forest". It is well known for its production of wooden glasses, skateboards and bamboo bikes. This company practices the craft to produce what it wishes to preserve the authentic aspect of its products and the local know-how while preserving at best the productivity of an industrial type company.

Their Website

My role in the company

In'Bô launches its new brand "Linotte", so for the release of their new product, they have prepared digital propaganda to highlight it and I am in charge of building the objects of this propaganda. It is a question of building wooden frames in which posters, frames/glasses displays as well as bases which emphasize the use of linen are arranged. This propaganda will be given to the opticians as gifts to put in their windows for the publication of Linotte.

Les Frères Pap's - Internship

May 8th - May 12th (2017)

"Les Frères Pap's" is a gastronomic restaurant located in Saint-Gilles, Reunion Island. Today it no longer exists and has been replaced by "La Villa Des Roches". I was able to exercise the role of waiter during one week. I discovered the specifications of the gastronomy, the preparation of the restaurant before the reception of the customers, and the presence of a waiter of this type of restaurant.
