Leisure Projects

Alone or with several people, I put my hobbies to the test by developing projects that I realize.

CPP-Connect

During our 2 years of preparatory class to the Grandes Ecoles (CPGE) we decided, two friends and myself, to collaborate for the development of a website proposing a multitude of tools to help the students of the future promotions and to learn the programming of Internet site.

Our website !

The creation of this website requires code in HTML and CSS obviously, then Javascript, Python, database management with MongoDB.

Website development is not only programming, it also requires design. You have to create images, decide on the style of the text and choose colors. So we made a lot of sketches before we started programming to get an idea of what we wanted to create.

Homemade Surfboard

During my summer vacations, with my passion for surfing and with a lot ahead of me, I had the idea to make my own surfboard from scratch using wood. This idea came to me at the end of my internship at In'Bô, because I love working with my hands. Once I had this idea in my head I couldn't get rid of it. So I started this ambitious project, just knowing that a surfboard has to float on water...

Before embarking on this rather ambitious project on my own, I did a lot of research on "how to build a surfboard". But most of my research led me to designs that used hard-to-find materials, and that didn't fit what I wanted to do. So I adapted what I could find on the internet in my own way. I decided to use wood to build my board and polystyrene so that it could float on water and not be too heavy.

Long moments of designing techniques to build this particular board appeared. I just wanted to use wood to make my board, without any experience. So I created a wooden skeleton as seen on this picture, and I filled it with polystyrene.

The project is still in progress!
